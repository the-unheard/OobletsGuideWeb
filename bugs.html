Known bugs and misreported bugs

NPCs and ooblets can sometimes be seen clipping through objects or each other.
Tud and Plob have duplicate figurine slots in the Town Hall.
Some cards have a different ooblet displayed on the art.
Some ooblets have the same cards. (Todoozies)
Some ooblets have the same seed model.
When using the hot air balloon, Gimble's Pantsabear gets left behind.

Dance Battle Bugs

Wigglewip's Plie DJ and Bristlebud's Stompulent beat cost stacks up when used on consecutive turns.
Cards that let you draw a new move may give you a non-signature card in Signature Only Tournament.
Multiples of Oogum's Backward Limbo can reduce warm-ups down to the negatives. They behave as 0-turn warm up.
When Whirlitzer's Gracing Pacing is reduced to 0-turn, the warm-up finishes instantly as it should, but the 2 extra beats do not get added until the next turn.
New If you steal then lower your beat cost, it will not affect the stolen card. If you lower your beat cost then steal, the stolen card will have lowered beat cost.
New When you have hype, you lose more points when using Wuddlin's and Hermble's moves.
New When you stun an already stunned ooblet, the stun counter displays the sum of the previous stun and the new stun. The counter fixes itself on the next turn though.
The stun counter should display either the new stun timer or the previous stun timer, whichever is higher.
New Hype and Fluster icon only stacks up to 5, while Trepidation icon stacks up infinitely.
New Three black triangles are floating in dance battles. It looks like left and right buttons.
New Using Sidekey's Finite Loop twice does not make the following move be played 3 times.

Other Bugs

When a received hand-held item goes to a slot you are already at, it becomes invisible. Going to another slot and back fixes it. (Not tested yet recently.)
Stamina does not recover when you do a complete halt after running. Walking triggers it.
When you a drop a huge quantity of an item and leave the area before it finishes, the rest of the items will disappear.
Home babies will stand still if they are spawned within a walled area, instead of exploring it.
New When you insert Strange Squares to the printer continuously, the "[Item] loaded" notification piles up until they cut off at the very top of the screen.
New Option becomes invisible after petting an ooblet from Ooblets > Manage > Options > Pet
New When an ooblet finishes a warm-up move, the camera zooms in at their back instead of the front.
New Game accepts keyboard input on the main menu even when set to gamepad input, and even though the game doesn't have focus (dual monitor).
New Wildlands give 10 Flooti Flour as rewards instead of 10 Muz Flour.
New When pressing B (gamepad) or ESC to refuse printing of a trinket/figurine, the entire printer menu closes.
New Game constantly auto-detects keyboard/gamepad and it pops a lot for gamepad users who often alt-tabs. There should be an option to disable auto-detection.

Ease of Life

It takes long to cook or make drinks when finishing multiple Plenny's orders.
It takes long to put hundreds of crops to the crunchster and swooshler.
It takes long for the circling animation to end when picking up an item, causing you to leave them behind during scene transition.
Manatee Shop does not stop you from buying multiples of the same paint, wallpaper, and tile.
Kibbonbon Shop displays the product you already bought instead of displaying the X sign.
New Home Decors menu does not show the names of wallpapers, tiles, and paints.
New Home Furnitures menu does not show the quantity of your furnitures.
New It can be difficult to find an ooblet in Grimboire and Wildlands without alphabetical / level sorting.
New No indicator if you received what item when using Snurfler or Plob.
New After a dance battle, it can take long for the game to show the exp gained by each ooblet one by one.

Author's Proposals

Wildlands should have true milestone requirements for leveling up instead of temporary milestones that can be abused by retrieving an ooblet after putting it.
See how many you already own when browsing furnitures in Manatee Shop.
New New keywords and icons for permanent buffs and debuffs such as Hand Decrease, Warmup Decrease, Beat Cost Increase/Decrease, Beats Per Turn.

Fixed

Lickzer and Wuddlin doesn't have figurine slots in the Town Hall.
NPCs fall off the cliff near Mossprouts Club. (Not a bug. There is a path that leads there.)
Spuddle's Risky Shift and Think Slink displays inaccurate points gained by the opponent when they have hype/fluster.
Skuffalo's Clean Quadrille does not work at all.
Chickadingding's Slipper Samba makes your opponent lose less points the more hype you have, and more points the more fluster you have.
Fleeble's Minuscule Medley does not stack with Firmo's Soft Shoe Servo.
Opening Grimboire Ooblets tab directly does not show any cursor selection. Going to another tab and back fixes it.
Home babies do not walk to the farm expansion.
When you retrieve or put an item to a chest, the tooltip remains.
When you upgrade an oobcoop, it says "Upgrade Oopcoop to level n?" where n is its current level. It should be n plus 1.
Farmers constantly lock the tiles when they are not doing anything.
The game only checks if your EXP can give you plus 1 level, only up to nine times at the end of the day, instead of a continuous loop to check if you can still level up.
It's difficult to find a specific ooblet when you have many oobcoops and they're all covered by their hats.
It's difficult to count the numbers of hype and fluster when there are too many.
It's difficult to interact with Dubble in Cuddlecups Cafe because the products are blocking her.
Suggestion: Show an icon for hype, fluster, and trepidation with counters next to them.
When there are too many hype and fluster, they will cut off on the screen.
Cards that increase beat costs does not work in Freebie Tournament.
